Katelyn Wong

Master's Student at UCSD Halicioglu Data Science Institute — Data Science, Math, Economics

Hi! I'm Katelyn Wong, a Master’s student in Data Science at UC San Diego, with a BS in Mathematics and Economics and a minor in Data Science. I’m passionate about data analysis, mathematical modeling, and applied research, using computational and statistical methods to tackle real-world problems.

I have experience in data science research and projects, including modeling energy data at UCSD’s Renewable Energy and Advanced Mathematics Lab, building predictive models for survival rates, user preferences, language prediction, and creating interactive data visualizations and developing my own coffee blog website from scratch.

Through my experiences working with the San Diego Association of Governments, UCSD's math department, and TritonHacks, I've strengthened my skills in algorithms, statistics, optimization, and web development, while also gaining valuable teamwork, problem-solving, and leadership experience through finance, mentoring, and administrative roles.

Outside of data science, I’m passionate about coffee and music. I run a coffee blog documenting cafe explorations and brewing techniques, and I enjoy producing and sharing music that inspires me. These hobbies reflect my curiosity, creativity, and dedication to exploring interests deeply.
